Let AI Do Complex Tasks For You

Most AI tools wait for your instructions step by step. AI agents are different. You give them a goal, and they figure out the steps on their own. They can browse websites, gather information, create documents, fill forms, and compile research without you guiding every action.

This category is promising but still early. Expect impressive demonstrations alongside real frustrations.
