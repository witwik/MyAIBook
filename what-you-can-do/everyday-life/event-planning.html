Event Planning and Social Coordination

Planning a birthday party, a group holiday, a family reunion, or any social event with multiple people and real constraints is exactly the kind of task where AI saves serious time. It handles the logistics. You handle the people.

"I am planning a 50th birthday dinner for my partner. We need a restaurant in [city/suburb] that can seat 20 people, has a private or semi-private dining area, offers a set menu option, has good vegetarian and gluten-free choices, and costs no more than $80 per head including drinks. We need it on a Saturday evening in [month]. Suggest three specific restaurants with approximate costs, what makes each one a good fit, and what I need to book or arrange in advance."
"I am organising a family reunion for approximately 30 people across three generations (ages 5 to 85). We need a venue within an hour of [city] that has indoor and outdoor space, is accessible for someone using a walking frame, has catering options or space to BYO food, and ideally has activities or space for kids to run around. Budget is $[amount] total. Suggest three options."
"Six friends and I are planning a long weekend away. We are based in different cities: [list cities]. We need a destination roughly equidistant, with accommodation for 6-8 adults, things to do that do not require a car once we arrive, and a total accommodation cost under $[amount] per person for 3 nights. Suggest three destinations with specific accommodation recommendations."

Try this right now (free)

Think of a social event you have coming up — even something small like a dinner for friends. Open Claude or ChatGPT and describe it with real constraints (number of people, budget, dietary requirements, location, date). See how quickly it produces a plan you can actually act on.
