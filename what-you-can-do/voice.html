Clone and Generate Voice

AI can now generate natural-sounding speech from text, and even clone your own voice from a short sample. This is useful for narration, accessibility, creating audio versions of written content, and creative projects.

ElevenLabs
What it isAI voice generation and cloning platform with natural-sounding speech synthesis
Best atText-to-speech in multiple voices and languages, voice cloning from short samples, audiobook narration
Free tierFree tier with limited characters per month and basic voices
First paid tierStarter Plan - US$5/month (~A$8/month)
Ken’s takeThe voice quality from ElevenLabs is startlingly natural. The free tier lets you test it. Voice cloning requires just a few minutes of audio and produces remarkably accurate results. The entry paid tier at US$5 is very affordable. Useful for anyone creating audio content, explainer videos, or accessibility versions of written material.
Sign uphttps://elevenlabs.io

Also worth knowing: NotebookLM’s audio overview feature (mentioned in the Research section) generates a two-person podcast discussion from your documents. It is not voice cloning, but it is an excellent way to turn written content into listenable audio, and it is free.

Try this right now (free)

Go to ElevenLabs and try the text-to-speech demo on their homepage. Paste a paragraph of text and listen to the different voice options. Notice how natural it sounds compared to the robotic text-to-speech you might be used to.
